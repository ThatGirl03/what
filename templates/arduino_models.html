Explore Our Arduino Models

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is one of the most popular and widely used microcontroller boards. It features an ATmega328P processor and is known for its ease of use, making it ideal for beginners and prototyping.

Arduino Mega

The Arduino Mega offers a larger number of input/output pins and more memory than the Uno. It's suited for complex projects that require more resources or multiple devices.

Arduino Nano

The Arduino Nano is a compact version of the Uno, with similar functionality but in a smaller form factor. It's ideal for projects with space constraints or for use on breadboards.

Arduino LilyPad

The Arduino LilyPad is designed for wearable electronics and e-textiles. It features a flexible PCB that can be sewn into fabric, making it ideal for creating smart clothing and accessories.

How to Update Your Details

To update your public profile, contact details, or bio on the Recycle Nexus platform, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account and navigate to the Nexus page.
  2. Click on the "Edit Profile" button to access your profile settings.
  3. Update your contact details, bio, and any other relevant information.
  4. Upload or change your profile picture and any images related to the materials you work with.
  5. Click "Save Changes" to update your information.
×

How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account from Recycle Nexus, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account and go to the Nexus page.
  2. Click on "Account Settings" or "Manage Account".
  3. Select "Delete Account" from the options.
  4. Confirm your decision to delete the account. Please note that this action is irreversible, and all your data will be permanently removed.

Understanding the Pages on Recycle Nexus

Nexus Page

The Nexus page allows you to:

  • Update Your Public Profile: Edit your contact details, bio, and information about what you recycle or the organization you work for.
  • Display Your Contact Details: Ensure your contact information is up-to-date and visible to others.
  • Upload and Manage Images: Add personal images or pictures of the materials you work with. This helps in showcasing your recycling activities or business.

R-Learning

The R-Learning page provides:

  • Articles and Links: Access resources that inform you about recycling, including what to recycle, reuse, and reduce.
  • Impact Information: Learn about the positive impacts of recycling and the potential negative consequences of not practicing it.

R-Trade

The R-Trade page allows you to:

  • Post Items: Share what you no longer need and choose from the following options:
    • Trade: Exchange materials with others.
    • Sell: Sell items for money.
    • Donate/Free: Offer items for free.
    • Request: Ask for specific materials and receive offers from potential sellers or donors.

Blog

The Blog page allows users to:

  • Post Stories: Share personal stories or experiences related to recycling.
  • React and Comment: Interact with other users' blog posts by reacting and commenting.
  • Content Moderation: We will guide and review the content for safety and appropriateness using integrated tools.

R-Videos

The R-Videos page enables users to:

  • View and Watch: Watch videos related to recycling or educational content.
  • Video Posting: Users can see who posted the video but cannot upload their own content.

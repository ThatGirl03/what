USB Cable

USB cables are used to connect computers and other devices for data transfer and charging. They come in various types such as USB-A, USB-B, USB-C, and micro-USB.

HDMI Cable

HDMI cables transmit high-definition video and audio between devices such as computers, TVs, and gaming consoles. They support high-quality resolution and sound.

Ethernet Cable

Ethernet cables are used for wired network connections. They connect devices to a network router or switch for reliable and high-speed internet access.

Jumper Wires

Jumper wires are used in electronics projects for connecting different components on a breadboard. They are available in various lengths and types (male-to-male, female-to-female, etc.).
